Indian war cabinet

Kashmir is a region primarily disputed between India and Pakistan, with China playing the third-party role. In the past few years, India has celebrated several military achievements owing to the efforts and bravery of our Jawans. But, attacks and skirmishes by militants have been a common sight in this region making it highly unsafe for citizens. The Indian war cabinet is a special committee which operates under total discretion during times of military crises. The fundamental responsibility of the committee is taking decisions during pressing times. The committee will try to solve the crisis at hand and also propose plans to prevent similar issues in the future. The Prime Minister and the National Security Advisor will be presiding the committee. The committee shall be attended by Important ministers, prominent opposition leaders, portfolios in the three wings of the armed forces and representatives of the Intelligence agencies of India. If you can work under pressure, utilise your critical thinking skills and can simultaneously plan strategies, this is the perfect committee for you!
