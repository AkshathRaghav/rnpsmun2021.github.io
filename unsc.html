SOCIAL, CULTURAL AND HUMANTARIAN COMMITTEE

“ NEVER lose faith in HUMANITY, as HUMANITY is an ocean; if a few drops of the ocean are dirty, doesn’t mean the rest of the ocean is dirty too."

Mahatma Gandhi


The Social, Cultural and humanitarian affairs committee (SOCHUM) is the Third Committee of the General Assembly of the United Nations and focuses broadly on the welfare and human rights of protected groups such as refugees, children, victims of discrimination, and many others. This committee deals with the most humane aspect of our societies. Violence against racially discriminated groups, the global fight for human rights, and a hope towards an anti-corrupt world is our TOP PRIORITY. The decisions of SOCHUM effect vital factors in the lives of the world community. Every Decision, Every Action, Every Struggle, Every War root from the society, culture, humanitarian values inculcated in people and their nations. The choices you make in this committee will have grave implications on life as a whole. Are you ready for that responsibility? Or Rather Are you ready to change the world? It is impossible to live without failing at something, unless you live so cautiously that you might as well have not lived at all.
