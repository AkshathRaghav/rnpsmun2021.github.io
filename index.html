About R-NPSMUN

NPS Rajajinagar proudly presents to you the first online edition of R-NPSMUN 2020!!!

An amalgamation of the consistent committees fused with a few unconventional ones, we have ensured that each delegate, new or experienced, has something to look forward to. Delegates will have to think on their feet to keep up with the pace of the debate, combating the obstacles posed, while sticking to the portfolios represented. Alliances will be made, strategies devised, while keeping the nations’ best interests at heart. Factual knowledge along with rational arbitrations will sail you through!

So delegates, come be a part of this exhilarating experience!

Secretary General

Anjana Palamand

Introducing your Secretary General for R-NPSMUN’20, Anjana Palamand  !

Anjana might look too enjoying politics, reading about feminism or minority’s rights, but behind that courteous smile and loud voice is a mind, thinking of how to pull everyone down. Having been to more than 20 conferences, she has carried along with her EB and secretariat positions, memories and trophies from all over the country. Trod slowly delegates, you might think she is walking right next to you, but she is far, far ahead of you.

Easily approachable and very affable, with her as your Secretary General, we hope all you delegates have a wonderful time at R-NPSMUN 2020!

Letter from the Secretery General

Greetings delegates!


From the RNPSMUN family, I extend a warm welcome to each and every one of you. I hope we find you in the best of health, for an online environment can be demanding for everyone, but holds opportunities for everyone to showcase their immense potential. As we meet on 10 September 2020, this MUN stands testimony to the truth that no change in external environment can deter a MUNner from learning, growing and enjoying!.

Five vibrant committees, a professional Executive Board, a very helpful Organising Committee and a determined Secretariat, this sums up all what a MUN needs to be a grand success. However, RMUN 2020 is more than just that. The efforts which have gone in for the past countless hours are only reflected in the EB’s and delegates’ happiness, and that is what I strive for. Regardless of whether you win or not, I promise you, you shall take back a learning experience and heartfelt memories like never before. Please note that I am always open to your feedback and/ or suggestions. We will make sure that there are no discrepancies from our side, however, if you still notice any, please feel free to reach out to me anytime. After attending more than twenty conferences, I have concluded that your allotment does not matter, your impact does. RMUN 2020, with a great sense of professionalism and experience, shall prove to you, exactly the same.

Looking forward to meeting all of you, and do not forget to have fun!


Anjana Palamand
Secretary General
R-NPSMUN 2020

Letter from the Director General

“Winning is something but participation is EVERYTHING ”

Greetings Delegates!

RNPSMUN has swung back into action again in 2020! Keeping the current circumstances in mind we have decided to go entirely virtual this year with five extremely exciting and well-rounded committees. Here we aim to channelise thinking beyond average range and make sure that every delegate receives all the motivation and guidance required to make this experience a delightful one..

I welcome all the first timers to the MUN circuit, knowing all about the nervousness and anticipation, trust me, it gets much better and all of you shall be amazing. Delegates feel free to voice your concerns and opinions without any hesitation at all. With that, best of luck and looking forward to an enthusiastic and humongous participation.

Srishti M
Crisis Director
